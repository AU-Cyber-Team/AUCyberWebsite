About Our Club

The Anderson University Cyber Team is a student-led organization dedicated to exploring the world of cybersecurity. We provide a space for students to learn, practice, and compete in various aspects of cyber defense and offense. From Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions to guest speakers and workshops, we're all about hands-on learning.
